Quotations and Citations

The HTML elements of the Quotation and Citation are "blockquote","q","cite", and "abbr".

BLOCKQUOTE

Below is a blockquote:

For 60 years, WWF has worked to help people and nature thrive. As the world's leading conservation organization, WWF works in nearly 100 countries. At every level, we collaborate with people around the world to develop and deliver innovative solutions that protect communities, wildlife, and the places in which they live.

Q

The Q tag defines a short quotation:

He said, and I quote,I cannot be stopped.

ABBR

The abbr tag defines an abbreviation:

HTML and CSS are front end languages.

ADDRESS

The address tag defined contact info:

The Pentagon.
Washington D.C.
USA

CITE

The cite tag usually defines the title of a creative work:

The Mona Lisa painted by Davinci.

BDO

BDO stands for Bi-Directionnal Override. It is used to override current text direction:

I own a racecar.
