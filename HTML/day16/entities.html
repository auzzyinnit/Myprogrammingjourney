Entities

Reserved HTML characters must be replaced with entities... e.g, less than = < and greater than = >

example of an entity name is greater than = >, while it's entity number is <. Obviously,names are easier to remember.

Non-breaking Space

The non breaking space entity is =  . This is a space that will not break into a new line.

Combining Diacritical Marks

A diacritical mark is a glyph added to a letter. examples include = à and ô
